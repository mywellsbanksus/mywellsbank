Mercer University and Wells Fargo have teamed up to add convenience to your active day with the unique custom-designed Mercer Bears Affinity Debit Card exclusively for Mercer University students, faculty, and staff. 

Order it.

Already have an account with Wells Fargo? Sign on to order your Mercer Bears Affinity Debit Card online for your Wells Fargo checking account.

Or order your Mercer Bears Affinity Debit Card when you open your Wells Fargo checking account at a Wells Fargo branch in Georgia. Find a branch near you and schedule an appointment.

On campus or off, you're ready. 

  • Easily make purchases at local retailers and restaurants, and for online services.
  • Access ATMs across the U.S., including no-fee cash access at Wells Fargo ATMs, including ATMs on campus.
  • Add your Mercer Bears Affinity Debit Card to your digital wallet to tap and pay.
  • Use the Wells Fargo Mobile® app to check account activity, deposit checks, transfer funds, pay bills, send money with Zelle®, and set up push notifications, text, or email alerts.

Link your Mercer Bears Affinity Debit Card to a Wells Fargo Clear Access Banking account for these exclusive benefits during each fee period:

  • No monthly service fee charged.
  • No Wells Fargo fees for up to four cash withdrawals from non-Wells Fargo ATMs in the U.S.
  • Courtesy refund of one incoming wire transfer fee.

Link your Mercer Bears Affinity Debit Card to an Everyday Checking account to receive the same benefits, as well as:

  • No fee charged for one overdraft.

Activate your Mercer Bears Affinity Debit Card when you receive it in the mail by calling the toll-free number provided, visiting wellsfargo.com/activatecard, or using any Wells Fargo ATM.

Additional protection feature

Your Wells Fargo Debit Card comes with Zero Liability protection at no extra cost. You will be reimbursed for any unathorized card transactions when reported promptly reported. Learn more at wellsfargo.com/debit-card.

Start your financial journey with checking accounts for students.

Choose between Clear Access Banking and Everyday Checking. Link your Campus Card to the account that best meets your needs. To learn about exclusive benefits when you link your Campus Card to a Clear Access Banking or Everyday Checking account, go to the My Card tab.

Those 17 years or older can choose between these two account options. Teens under 18 must open in a branch. See what you'll need to open a Clear Access Banking account, or what you'll need for Everyday Checking.

With Extra Day Grace Period, if your account is overdrawn, you have an additional business day (extra day) to make covering deposits and/or transfers to avoid overdraft fees. If your available balance as of 11:59 pm Eastern Time on your extra day is positive, the pending overdraft fees for the prior business day’s overdraft items will be waived. If your available balance as of 11:59 pm Eastern Time is enough to cover some, but not all, of the prior business day’s overdraft items, the available balance will be applied to the transactions in the order that they posted to your account (based on our posting order practices described in the Deposit Account Agreement). Any overdraft items that are not covered by 11:59 pm Eastern Time on your extra day are subject to applicable overdraft fees.
