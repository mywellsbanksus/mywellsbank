The Wells Fargo Campus CardSM Program — For Administrators

  • Print

Enjoy choice and convenience with the
Wells Fargo Campus CardSM Program

With the Wells Fargo Campus Card Program, your university or college can offer students, faculty, and staff members a valuable optional banking service. With our program, you can:

  • Provide optional banking access with on- and off-campus convenience for your campus community.
  • Choose from card program options that work for your campus community's needs.
  • Team up with a proven provider familiar with what universities and colleges are looking for in a campus card program.

Your school can benefit from the program, with:

  • Card issuance options. Retain your on-campus card issuance control or have Wells Fargo manage it for you.
  • Banking access. Students, faculty, and staff will enjoy access to approximately  4,600   branches and more than  11,000 ATMs nationwide.
  • Dedicated support. Local and national relationship management representatives work directly with your team.

Why choose Wells Fargo?

  • Experience. For more than 20 years, we've provided campus banking services at schools from coast to coast .
  • Customized marketing. We can help you create marketing options that highlight your program’s benefits.
  • Financial education resources. Access to online and mobile tools, such as the Hands on Banking® program and My Financial Guide , as well as in person and virtual financial workshops and webinars. Especially for students, parents and counselors, Wells Fargo's CollegeSTEPS® has guidance to help students learn how to prepare and pay for school, understand credit and manage money with confidence in college and life.
  • Products and services. Students, faculty, and staff can enjoy a variety of products and services that may help meet their financial needs.

To learn more about the Wells Fargo Campus Card Program, please contact Vernon Sloan at vernon.sloan@wellsfargo.com

Wells Fargo may provide financial support to universities or colleges with the Wells Fargo Campus Card program.
