The Wells Fargo Campus CardSM Program

  • Print

No campus is quite like yours. No program is quite like ours.®

The Wells Fargo Campus Card Program offers optional banking services to students at participating colleges and universities. These services are also available for faculty and staff at most programs. Visit our participating school list for additional details.

Enjoy the convenience of accessing your cash at ATMs and making purchases on and off campus if you choose to link your Wells Fargo checking account to the:

  • Campus ATM Card

    This school-issued student ID serves as an official campus ID card as well as an ATM card, when it's linked to a Wells Fargo checking account, all in one. Use this card to access campus privileges (meal plans, library access, etc.), for optional banking access to ATMs, and to make purchases using a Personal Identification Number (PIN).

  • Campus Affinity Debit Card

    This optional, custom, co-branded Visa® debit card issued by Wells Fargo is designed and available for your school. Use the card to access ATMs and to make purchases. Note: There is no campus-access feature with this card.

Banking options, access, and benefits

With a choice of accounts that can be linked to Campus Cards for added banking convenience, the checking accounts we offer provide flexibility so each individual can reach their financial goals with the option that works for them.

Choose from checking accounts for students, including accounts with exclusive benefits when linked to your Campus Card.

Link your Campus Card to a Wells Fargo Clear Access Banking account for these exclusive benefits during each fee period:

  • No monthly service fee charged.
  • No Wells Fargo fees for up to four cash withdrawals from non-Wells Fargo ATMs in the U.S.
  • Courtesy refund of one incoming wire transfer fee.

Link your Campus Card to an Everyday Checking account to receive the same benefits during each fee period, as well as:

  • No fee charged for one overdraft.

Banking with Wells Fargo and your Wells Fargo Campus Card gives you access to additional services such as:

  • The Wells Fargo Mobile® app to check account activity, deposit checks, transfer funds, pay bills, send money with Zelle®, and set up push notifications, text, or email alerts.
  • Helpful online financial educational resources and money management tools.
  • CollegeSTEPS® planning resources, including access to a Scholarship Resource Center, and more.
