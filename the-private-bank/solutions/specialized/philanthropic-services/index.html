Philanthropic Services

  • Print

Deepen your charitable impact

For over 50 years, Wells Fargo Bank has been working with individuals, families, and nonprofit organizations to help them achieve their strategic charitable goals. With over $25 billion* in assets under management, 100 dedicated professionals located throughout the United States, and a comprehensive suite of specialized charitable solutions, Wells Fargo Bank Philanthropic Services has the experience and commitment to help bring your vision to life. From structuring lifetime giving programs to handling the day-to-day operations of a large-scale foundation or nonprofit organization, our specialists can meet you wherever you are on your philanthropic journey and help you take your next steps.
