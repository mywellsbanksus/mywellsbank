Business Rewards FAQs

How do I access, view, or redeem my rewards? How do I access, view, or redeem my rewards?
Online
  • Sign on to Business Online Banking. You can view your rewards balance on the Account Summary page. To view additional details or redeem, select Wells Fargo Business Rewards.
Phone
  • Call the Business Card Rewards Service Center at 1-800-213-3365, 8:00 a.m. - 12 midnight, Eastern Time, seven days a week (except major holidays). 
  • NOTE: Have your Rewards Account Number (Rewards ID) available when you are ready to redeem. You can always find your Rewards ID on your monthly statement (listed as Membership Number).
Can employees earn rewards? Can employees earn rewards?
Yes, eligible transactions on employee accounts earn rewards and will accumulate on the primary cardholder’s account.
Can I set up someone to manage rewards on my behalf? Can I set up someone to manage rewards on my behalf?

Yes. Business owners that use Business Online Banking can grant online access to someone using an online feature called Business Administrator*. This feature enables a Business Administrator to manage rewards as well as view account information and perform transactions. To access this feature, sign on to Business Online Banking and select Manage Access from the Welcome menu. Then choose Manage Business Administrators.

*Not available for Wells Fargo VantageSM customers.

How can I learn more about the Business Rewards program? How can I learn more about the Business Rewards program?
Review the Wells Fargo Business Rewards Program Terms and Conditions.
Call Wells Fargo Business Rewards Customer Service at 1-800-213-3365, 8:00 a.m. - 12 midnight, Eastern Time, seven days a week (except major holidays).

How was your experience? Give us feedback.

Rewards redeemed to an eligible Wells Fargo business credit product are applied toward your principal balance. You must still make regularly scheduled payments.

Rewards items and redemption values are subject to change at any time.
