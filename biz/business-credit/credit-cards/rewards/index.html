⁠⁠Business Rewards FAQs
⁠

How do I earn rewards points?

Earn one point for every dollar spent on qualifying purchases made with your eligible Wells Fargo Business credit card or Mastercard® access card. There is no limit to the number of base rewards points you earn.

How do I access, view, or redeem my rewards?

Online

  • Business Banking customers: Sign on to Business Online Banking. You can view your points balance on the Account Summary page. To view additional details or redeem, select Wells Fargo Business Rewards.
  • Commercial customers: Sign on to Wells Fargo VantageSM and select Basic Banking. Access Account Details and enter or select the credit card account enrolled in the Business Rewards Points Program. Click the Business Rewards Program Website link.

Phone

  • Call the Business Card Rewards Service Center at 1-800-213-3365, 8:00 am - 12 midnight, Eastern Time, seven days a week (except major holidays).

    NOTE:     Have your Rewards Account Number (Rewards ID) available when you are ready to redeem. You can always find your Rewards ID on your monthly statement.

How long will it take to receive rewards that I order?

Gift Cards are mailed within 7-10 business days of your redemption order. All merchandise items are subject to availability and will usually ship within 3-7 business days from the date of the request. Digital gift cards are delivered electronically via email within 1-3 business days of the order.

Do my rewards points expire?

Points will expire five years from the month in which they were earned. You can view expiring points on your monthly statements. Points are redeemed on a first-in, first-out basis. Expired points will not be reinstated under any circumstances.

Can employees earn rewards points?

Yes. Purchases made on employee credit cards earn rewards, which are available to the primary cardholder for redemption.

If your company is set up with individual billing, points earned from employee cards may roll up to the rewards owner’s account at cycle time with a one-month lag.

Can I set up someone to manage rewards on my behalf?

Yes. Business owners that use Business Online Banking can grant online access to someone using an online feature called Business Administrator*. This feature enables a Business Administrator to manage rewards as well as view account information and perform transactions.

To access this feature, sign on to Business Online Banking and select Manage Access from the Welcome menu. Then choose Manage Business Administrators.

* Not available for Business Lines of Credit or Wells Fargo VantageSM customers.

Can I transfer points to another Wells Fargo Rewards program?

Yes. You can transfer points between two eligible business products or between eligible business and personal products. Visit your Business Rewards homepage, click "My Rewards" in the top navigation menu and select "Transfer".

How can I learn more about the Business Rewards program?

Review the Wells Fargo Business Rewards Program Terms and Conditions.

Call Wells Fargo Business Rewards Customer Service at 1-800-213-3365, 8:00 am - 12 midnight, Eastern Time, seven days a week (except major holidays).

Rewards redeemed as a credit to an eligible Wells Fargo business credit product are applied toward your principal balance. You must still make regularly scheduled payments.

Rewards items and redemption values are subject to change at any time.

Flights must be redeemed through the Wells Fargo Business Rewards website or service center. Certain restrictions and limitations apply.

You will earn one point for every dollar spent on qualifying purchases (purchases less returns and credits, and excluding balance transfers, cash advances, or any fees or interest posted to the account) for the life of the account. Refer to the Wells Fargo Business Rewards Program Terms and Conditions for more information about the rewards program.
