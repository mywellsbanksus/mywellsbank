3. How You Earn Rewards

Rewards accrual will begin on the date You open Your account or enroll in the Program, depending on Your credit card. No retroactive Rewards will be awarded.

If You have multiple cardholders in Your company and the company is set up with individual bill, Rewards earned from employee cards will roll up to Your account with a one-month delay in billing cycle. If Your company is set up with consolidated bill, the Rewards earned by employee cards will appear in the rewards owner’s account (consolidated bill account) without a one-month billing cycle delay.

Rewards are updated monthly at the time Your Credit Card account statement is produced. Purchases that have not yet appeared on a monthly billing statement are not eligible for Rewards redemption.

The following purchases/transactions do not earn Rewards: purchases made using a PIN, ATM transactions, cash advances of any kind, balance transfers, SUPERCHECKSTM, casino gaming chips, wire transfers, off-track wagers, lottery ticket transactions, or bets or wagers transmitted over the Internet. Any fees posted to a linked account, including but not limited to finance charges, and the non-refundable annual fee, if applicable, do not earn Rewards.

a. Base Rewards Earn:

Based on Your Credit Card Product and Rewards type, Cardholders will earn:

  • For Points Rewards Cards: One point for every $1 spent in qualifying purchases (purchases minus returns/credits) made on their Credit Card account, rounded to the nearest whole dollar for Points Rewards cards.
  • For Cash Rewards Cards: Two cents ($0.02) Cash Rewards for every $1 spent in qualifying purchases (purchases minus returns/credits), made on their Credit Card account, rounded to the nearest one cent.

Purchases made using a Personal Identification Number (PIN) do not earn Rewards. Any credits, except payments to the account, will reduce the number of Rewards available for redemption based on the dollar amount of the credit. Negative Rewards will post within 1-2 billing periods on Your account statement if returns or credits exceed purchases.

b. Monthly Bonus Rewards Earn:

For Points Rewards Customers: You and eligible employee cardholders can also earn:

  • Business Card: 1,000 bonus Points per each monthly billing cycle that the company spends at least $1,000. Bonus Points will post in the billing cycle after the $1,000 spend threshold is achieved.
  • Business Elite Card: 5,000 bonus Points per each monthly billing cycle that the company spends at least $10,000. Bonus Points will post in the billing cycle after the $10,000 spend threshold is achieved.
  • Business Line/Small Business Advantage MasterCard Access Card: 1,000 bonus Points per each monthly billing cycle that the company spends at least $1,000. Bonus Points will post in the billing cycle after the $1,000 spend threshold is achieved.

c. Online Redemption Bonus:

For Points Rewards Customers: Online redemptions (made through the Business Rewards website) will receive a 10% Points credit in number of Points (10% of redemption value), which will be credited to your account the following month. The 10% Points credit is not applicable for redemptions made through the Wells Fargo Business Rewards Call Center at 1-800-213-3365.

4. Annual Rewards Cap Maximums:

There is no limit to the number of Rewards You can earn.

5. Points Expiration

For Points Rewards Customers: Points will expire sixty (60) months from the month in which they were originally earned. Ensure you redeem the Points before expiration. You can find details regarding your upcoming expiring Points at wellsfargo.com/businessrewards. Points are redeemed on a first-in, first-out basis. Expired Points will not be reinstated under any circumstances.

NOTICE TO NEW YORK ACCOUNT HOLDERS: When there are material changes to the Wells Fargo Business Rewards Program, all Rewards expiration will be paused for 90 days after the effective date of the material change. For example, when a Change In Terms occurs in January, Points set to expire on January 31st would not expire until April 30th.

6. Bill Payments and Recurring Payments

  • If You choose to use Your credit card to fund Your bill payments through the Wells Fargo Online Bill Payment service, You will not earn Rewards.
  • If You contact a merchant directly and set up a recurring payment to charge Your Business Card directly You will earn Rewards based upon the above-stated calculation.
