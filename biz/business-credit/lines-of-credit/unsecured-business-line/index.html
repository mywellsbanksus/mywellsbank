Wells Fargo Unsecured Business Lines of Credit

  • Print

Wells Fargo BusinessLine® line of credit for customers in business two or more years

  • New customer offer: No annual fee for the first year
  • $10,000 to $150,000 revolving credit line
  • Rates as low as Prime + 1.75%
  • Automatic enrollment in a free rewards program

Wells Fargo Small Business Advantage® line of credit for customers in business less than two years

  • No annual fee
  • $5,000 to $50,000 revolving credit line
  • Rates as low as Prime + 4.50%
  • Automatic enrollment in a free rewards program

Things to consider when preparing to apply for an unsecured business line of credit:

  • Typically, guarantors have a FICO® Score of at least 680 at time of application.
  • A BusinessLine line of credit account requires personal guarantees from any owner with 25% or more ownership in the applicant business, with a minimum combined aggregate of 51% ownership.
  • A Small Business Advantage line of credit account requires personal guarantees from any owner with 20% or more ownership in the applicant business, with a minimum combined aggregate of 51% ownership.
  • Additional information related to applying for credit can be found at our Small Business Resource Center:

    • Developing a good credit application
    • Five steps to consider before applying for credit

All business applicants are unique and some exceptions may apply. All applications are subject to final credit approval.

BusinessLine line of credit:

  • For customers in business two or more years.

Details

  • Amounts. Credit lines from $10,000 to $150,000.
  • Terms. Revolving line of credit, no scheduled annual review.
  • Rates. Rates as low as Prime + 1.75%; interest charged when you use the account.
  • Collateral. None needed.
  • Guarantors. Requires personal guarantees from any owner with 25% or more of the applicant business, and a minimum combined aggregate of 51% ownership.

Fees

  • Annual fee. The annual fee is waived for the first year only. An annual fee of $95 (if line amount is $10,000 - $25,000) or $175 (if line amount is more than $25,000) will be assessed on your anniversary month in subsequent years.
  • Cash Advance Fee. No fee for access checks, online transfers, Bill Pay or telephone transfers. 3% ($10 minimum) fee for ATM and over the counter transactions from a bank or financial institution. 4% ($10 minimum) fee for wire transfer advances and for Casino Cash/Quasi Cash transactions when using the BusinessLine® Mastercard® access card.

Customer Agreement

Small Business Advantage line of credit – backed by U.S. Small Business Administration (SBA)

  • For customers in business less than two years.

Details

  • Amounts. Credit lines from $5,000 to $50,000.
  • Terms. 5-year revolving line of credit, no scheduled annual review.
  • Rates. Rates as low as Prime + 4.50%; interest charged when you use the account.
  • Collateral. None needed.
  • Guarantors. Requires personal guarantees from any owner with 20% or more of the applicant business, and a minimum combined aggregate of 51% ownership.
  • Must meet U.S. SBA requirements. Household personal liquid assets less than $500,000. For-Profit businesses only.

Fees

  • Annual fee. No annual fee.
  • Cash Advance Fee. No fee for ATM, over the counter transactions from a bank or financial institution, access checks, online transfers, Bill Pay or telephone transfers. 4% ($10 minimum) fee for wire transfer transactions and for Casino Cash/Quasi Cash transactions when using the Small Business Advantage Mastercard® access card.
