Discover the all-new RUN Powered by ADP® (RUN), payroll and HR solutions for small business.

Faster

  • Payroll in a flash - RUN can automatically process your payroll for you. Just confirm and you’re done.
  • Intelligent search - A smart search tool with auto suggestions instantly connects you to popular resources, favorite reports, frequent tasks and more.
  • Find qualified candidates – Smart-matching technology helps you post to 100+ job boards and sites in just one click.

Smarter
