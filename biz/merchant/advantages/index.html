Advantages of Credit Card Payment Processing

  • Print

Expand your payment options, and your business

Our Merchant Services solutions can help you accept card payments from your customers onsite, online, or on the go.

View options

Take card payments with customizable payment solutions that grow with your business, from invoicing to online payments to point-of-sale processing and customer subscriptions.

Key advantages

  • Get paid fast – as soon as the next business day – when you deposit into a Wells Fargo business deposit account.
  • Robust capabilities that help minimize time spent on payments with convenient set-up and integration with other software and systems.
  • Online account management tools let you view your transaction activity, create and schedule reports, quickly manage disputes, and research customer card data online 24/7.
  • Data Security and PCI Compliance support to help you manage your compliance with PCI-Data Security Standards.
  • Straightforward pricing makes it easier to estimate your monthly processing costs.
  • Experienced Merchant Services specialists can get you set up quickly and provide ongoing support as you need it. You can also access live, U.S.-based service specialists 24/7.

Grow your business

We have the experience to help meet the changing needs of your business at every stage, from getting started to opening a new location and expanding into new channels.

  • Close more sales. Provide your customers multiple ways to pay by accepting debit and credit cards, contactless payments, phone and website orders.
  • Access working capital. Our Business Loans and Lines of Credit and Business Credit Cards offer a range of solutions for merchants to get access to working capital.
  • Attract corporate and government customers. Enable your point of sale terminal to accept purchase card data (p-cards) to attract and retain corporate and government customers.
  • Add more locations. We can help you scale up and provide additional support to meet increasing processing needs.

Processing and as soon as next business day funding

When a customer makes a payment, a number of technologies and institutions work together to approve the transaction in seconds. If you use Wells Fargo Merchant Services with a Wells Fargo business deposit account, you’ll get paid as soon as the next business day.

Here’s how payment processing works

1. Customer makes an electronic payment.

2. Merchant terminal (in person) or gateway (online) electronically sends an authorization request to the customer’s issuing bank.

3. Customer’s bank approves or declines the transaction.

4. Authorization or decline is sent back to the terminal or internet gateway notifying the merchant and customer of status.

5. Merchant sends transaction for settlement to payment processor.

6. Payment processor deposits funds into the merchant’s account.
