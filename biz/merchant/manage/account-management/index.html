For merchants processing payments through any Clover device:

Clover Dashboard provides a view of all your business's information in one place, in real-time. It's an effective way to keep your sales, employees' performance, inventory, Clover apps, and business goals – all organized in one central location. Visualize KPIs, track performance, and see data directly within the dashboard to analyze trends and gain insights from what you see daily, weekly, and/or monthly. Learn more about the key benefits, available applications and tools here.

To sign in or enroll in Clover Dashboard, visit www.clover.com/login

For merchants processing payments through other terminals, gateways and ISVs:

Commerce Control Center is a secure online account management and reporting portal that helps you track, analyze and monitor your business' payment processing and settlement activity. You can also manage chargebacks and disputes and access statement and tax information.

To sign in or enroll in Commerce Control Center, visit www.commercecontrol.com.

Key benefits and features

  • Access statements securely and conveniently. View and download up to 7 years of statements online without waiting for the mail.
  • Quickly resolve disputes and chargebacks. Use the Dispute ManagerSM application, within Commerce Control Center, to review, manage, and respond to requests for information online, instead of by mail or phone.
  • Receive key account updates. Customize and schedule reporting on disputes, chargebacks, next-day sales, funding and more.
  • Analyze data and activity. Use the reporting tool features to perform historical and trend analyses of your transactional activity to guide business strategies and streamline account reconciliation.
  • Generate tax reporting. Get tax validation, gross reportable sales reports, and copies of your 1099-K forms — or choose only to receive an annual tax document by enrolling in Form 1099-K eConsent.
  • Integrate reporting. Set up APIs that integrate into your website or portal that enable access to specific real-time datasets.

User tools

  • Manage preferences. Choose the applications you'd like to use and manage your profile and account settings.
  • View summaries. Customize views of data for authorizations, settlements, funding, chargebacks, gross reportable sales and your Backup Withholding Summary.
  • Access resources. Engage in self-paced training, review reporting samples and find FAQs.

Existing merchant customers:

Call us at 1-800-451-5817 for live, U.S.-based service, 24/7. Or contact your account manager.
