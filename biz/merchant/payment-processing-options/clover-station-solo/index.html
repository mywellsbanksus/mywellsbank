Clover® Station Solo

Accept payments with a robust point-of-sale system

The Clover Station Solo helps you manage transactions and staff with ease

To apply, call 1-866-609-3378 or request a call

When one touchscreen is all you need

  • One-time device cost: $1,720
  • Software plans: Starting at $49.95 per month
  • Monthly service fee: $9.95 per month
  • Transaction fees: See "Fees" tab below

Features Features

When you open a Wells Fargo Merchant Services account with a Clover Station Solo, you can:

  • Accept all credit and debit cards, cash, and checks
  • Take payments online and over the phone with a virtual terminal
  • Track and manage orders and staff
  • Expect Point-to-Point Encryption (P2PE) and tokenization
  • Connect via Wi-Fi and Ethernet
  • Integrate with accounting software and customize your system with a wide selection of apps from Clover App Market

With all Wells Fargo Merchant Services accounts, you'll get paid as soon as the next business day when you deposit into a Wells Fargo business deposit account.

Equipment Equipment
  • 14" display
  • Built-in camera for barcode and QR scanning
  • Cash drawer
  • Receipt printer
  • Optional add-ons include barcode scanner, label printer, weight scale, kitchen printers and kitchen displays
Software Software

Available software plans for Clover Station Solo are listed below. You will be asked to select the plan that best meets your needs when you activate your device. Please note Clover Go devices can be added to your account without an additional monthly fee.

Register plan

  • Designed for busier retail and restaurant businesses
  • Process payments, manage employees, create invoices and reports, plus enhanced inventory and order management capabilities
  • $49.95 per month for the first device; $19.95 per month for each additional device

Counter Service Restaurant plan

  • Designed for counter and quick-service restaurants
  • Create menu items, send orders to the kitchen, and use food delivery apps
  • $59.95 per month for the first device; $19.95 per month for each additional device  

Table Service Restaurant plan

  • Tailored for table service restaurants
  • Includes Counter Service plan features, plus ability to map tables, open tabs, and split bills
  • $89.95 per month for the first device; $19.95 per month for each additional device 

You may further customize your device to help meet your business needs with hundreds of apps.

Fees Fees

No application fees, no monthly minimums, and no early termination fees

Monthly service fee: $9.95 per month, per location

  • 24/7 live support
  • Reporting and analytics
  • Advanced risk and fraud monitoring
  • Tools to help you manage disputes

Transaction fees
Your rates will automatically adjust based on your processing volume each month. Custom (Interchange Plus) is also available for select businesses. Call 1-866-609-3378 for more information.

Business-to-consumer (B2C) transaction fees
Merchants like restaurants, retailers, health practitioners, and any business focused on selling to consumers. Based on monthly processing volume:

$0 - $14,999.99
Per tap, dip, or swipe: 2.60% + $0.15
Per online or keyed in: 3.40% + $0.15

$15,000 - $39,999.99

Per online or keyed in: 3.20% + $0.15

$40,000 and up

Per online or keyed in: 3.10% + $0.15
