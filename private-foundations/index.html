Wells Fargo Trust Philanthropic Services Private Foundations

  • Print

As part of the Wealth & Investment Management division, Trust Philanthropic Services provides a full array of products and services through Wells Fargo Bank, N.A. to private and family foundations across the country, including grant making support. For these foundations, Wells Fargo Bank serves as sole trustee, co-trustee or agent.

To serve these foundations, we seek to help non-profit organizations identify appropriate grant resources for specific funding needs. To help you find these grants, we provide a wide range of information about these foundations through our search feature.
