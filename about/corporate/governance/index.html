Code of Conduct

Wells Fargo’s reputation as one of the world’s great companies for integrity and principled performance depends on our doing the right thing, in the right way, and complying with the laws, rules and regulations that govern our business. We earn trust by behaving ethically and holding all employees and directors accountable for the decisions we make and the actions we take. The Code of Conduct (PDF) serves to guide the actions and decisions of our employees, including executive officers, and directors.
