Jason Rosenberg

  • Print

Senior Executive Vice President, Head of Public Affairs

Wells Fargo & Company

Jason Rosenberg joined Wells Fargo in 2024 as Head of Public Affairs, which includes the company’s Corporate Communications, Corporate Marketing, Government Relations and Public Policy, Philanthropy and Community Impact, and Sustainability groups. Jason is a member of Wells Fargo’s Operating Committee.

During his career, Jason has held numerous roles in both the private and public sectors. Prior to joining Wells Fargo, he was Head of Corporate Affairs at Block, Inc., where he led a global team comprised of Policy, Corporate and Internal Communications and Creative Studio, Block’s internal creative agency.

Jason also held various roles at JPMorgan Chase during his decade-long tenure there, most recently serving as Head of US Government Relations and as a member of the Corporate Responsibility Management Team, which oversees global philanthropy, impact finance, sustainability, community engagement, research and policy, as well as global government relations.

Jason's public-sector service experience includes working in the United States Senate and United States House of Representatives, including as Senior Economic Policy Advisor to a member of the Senate Committee on Banking, Housing, and Urban Affairs during the 2008 financial crisis.

Jason is a graduate of Syracuse University.
