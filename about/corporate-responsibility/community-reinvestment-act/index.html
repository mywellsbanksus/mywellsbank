  • Print

CRA public file

Wells Fargo helps to meet the credit needs of the communities we serve, including low- and moderate-income neighborhoods, in alignment with the Community Reinvestment Act (CRA). We're providing the information in this public file under CRA regulations. If you have any questions, contact the CRA Strategy and Program Management team at CRAPublicFileRequest@wellsfargo.com.
