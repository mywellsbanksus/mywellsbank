Industry experience that helps to enhance your customer experience

As a leading bank for more than 35,000 customers involved with Agribusiness production, and processing, our depth of experience allows us to provide our customers with valuable insights and a true competitive advantage. 

We have a keen understanding of the challenges and variables inherent within the industry including agronomic cycles, seasonality, weather, labor, transportation, technology, safety, trade, and government regulation, along with the impact these challenges have on your bottom line. What's more, we also provide the resources to help you manage a wide spectrum of risk.

Industry leadership

  • One of the top commercial bank agricultural lenders in the U.S. for 25 consecutive years
  • Over $30 billion in commitments to over 35,000 Agribusiness customers throughout the U.S. across all sectors

Local relationships that go further

Our Agribusiness and Food Processing specialized industry group brings together the benefits of exceptional local service and extensive industry experience. Our bankers live, work, and serve in the same communities as you. They take the time to get to know you, your business operations, and your priorities. And, they’re empowered to share every advantage of collective resources Wells Fargo can offer.

Capabilities when you need them

Wells Fargo provides customized Agribusiness solutions that draw from one of the broadest ranges of products and services in the industry, including:

  • Crop and feed lines of credit
  • Livestock, poultry, and dairy lines of credit
  • Working capital lines of credit
  • Term financing
  • Real estate financing
  • Equipment financing
  • Treasury management services
  • Farm and ranch asset management
  • International trade
  • Foreign exchange
  • Commodity hedging
  • Interest rate hedging
  • Investment banking services
  • Futures clearing
  • Business valuation and succession planning
