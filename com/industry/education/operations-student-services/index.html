Higher Education Operations

  • Print

Aim to manage your operations more efficiently

With our deep industry experience and knowledge, we can help you with all facets of your operations. Some of our key capabilities include:

  • Credit Services. Direct pay letters of credit, standby bond purchase agreements, direct purchase loans and lines of credit.
  • Treasury Management. Accelerate your receivables, control your payables, make better use of cash, and control fraud.
  • Merchant Services. Streamline your credit card, debit card, and check payment operations.
  • International Services. Wire transfers and international payment services to support study abroad programs, visiting faculty, and more.
  • Employee Banking. Offer your employees our deposit and credit products, plus financial education resources.
  • Investment Management. Get institutional investment management help for your endowment, foundation, and balance sheet assets.
  • Refund Disbursement Services. May reduce operating expenses by effectively managing the distribution of refunds to students. Our secure website lets students manage how they receive their refunds, helping to ensure their refunds arrive promptly, safely, and accurately.

Our commitment to educational institutions

We understand that higher education institutions are looking for more than a bank—you need a partner who can help you address the needs of your constituents and your institution. Our team of experienced Education Banking professionals will work closely with you to deliver a wide range of services to your faculty, staff, students, parents, and local community members.
