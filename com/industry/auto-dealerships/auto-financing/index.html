We aim to make funding simple and reliable.

Auto financing should run just as smoothly as the vehicle. Our credit products are designed to help qualified applicants feel like they’re already in the driver’s seat.

Here’s how it works:

  • Once a credit application is received in our system, the best financing option for a customer is determined. (Financing options are based on customer needs and ability to pay.)
  • Dealers receive updates on customer applications by credit and rate tiers.
  • Application status (Approved, Pending, or Submitted for Funding) can be tracked in real time on Dealertrack® or RouteOne®

Our Dealer Desk is dedicated to helping your business operate smoothly and can assist you with things like:

  • Dealer reserve statements
  • Lien releases
  • Loan unwinds
  • VIN corrections
  • Substitution of collateral
  • Aftermarket cancellations
  • Booksheet discrepancies
  • General questions
