McDonald's® Financing Program for Owner/Operators

Specialized lending for franchisees

For more than 160 years, Wells Fargo has offered companies the resources they need to succeed through economic and business cycles. And our Financing Program for McDonald's® Owner/Operators leverages the strength and stability of Wells Fargo to McDonald's franchisees nationwide.

Wells Fargo Restaurant Finance can customize financing to help fit your business needs. Our Restaurant Finance specialists will work with you to help ensure you receive optimal financing for your unique needs. Our products include:

  • Fixed and floating rate loans
  • New unit financing
  • Acquisition financing
  • Equipment revolvers
  • Rebuilds/Co-investments
  • Treasury management solutions
  • Wealth management services
