About Railcar Leasing and Financing

  • Print

Quality fleet, financial strength, exceptional people

Wells Fargo Rail operates one of the largest, most diverse railcar and locomotive fleets of any rail equipment operating lessor in North America with over 135,000 railcars and 850 locomotives. Our team of experienced rail industry professionals is ready to listen to your needs and design and deliver creative and competitive equipment solutions. Wells Fargo Rail — backed by the strength and experience of Wells Fargo — wants to satisfy our customers’ rail equipment needs and help them succeed financially.

Whatever you’re transporting, we’ve got you covered. Drawing from our vast network of contacts and resources across the U.S., Canada, and Mexico, we can quickly locate the exact type of equipment you’re looking for and tailor a leasing program to your specifications.

Our dedicated Wells Fargo Rail team will work with you to understand your business goals and support them with experience and products and services such as equipment modifications, equipment maintenance, customized lease terms, a variety of leasing options, railcar accounting, equipment management, flexible rates and financing.
