Mergers and Acquisitions

Unique industry M&A industry insights with a global strategic perspective

Expert advisory services

Wells Fargo Mergers & Acquisitions group provides customized advice to help our clients address complex strategic challenges and meet their objectives, both near and long-term.

Our clients include a broad range of privately-owned and publicly traded corporations, private individuals and families, private equity and other institutional investors, and governments.

We design and execute bespoke, innovative solutions for our clients involving domestic and cross-border transactions including:

  • Mergers
  • Acquisitions
  • Leveraged buyouts
  • Company sales
  • Carve-outs, divestitures, and spinoffs
  • Recapitalizations
  • Joint ventures and partnerships
  • Activism and takeover defense
  • Shareholder relations

Our M&A team includes transactions structuring experts and senior bankers with significant negotiations and deal tactics expertise.

We collaborate with experts across Wells Fargo to deliver the full range of industry insights, products, and services required by our clients. We work with our market-leading Debt Capital Markets, Derivatives, Leveraged Finance, and Equity Capital Markets teams to assist clients as they access hedge risks and capital required for M&A transactions.

Our Mergers & Acquisitions team is located across nine offices in the United States, Europe, and Asia.
