We understand the challenges and opportunities that today's Japanese institutional investors face. We focus on helping our customers simplify their investment process, optimize returns and manage risk. 

We provide access to the U.S. fixed income markets and insightful strategies for a broad range of customers who manage all or a portion of their own investment portfolio.

Wells Fargo Securities Japan serves large financial institutional clients, consisting of megabanks, trust banks, life insurance companies, government-related entities, and asset managers.
