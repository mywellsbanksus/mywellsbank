Celebrating Black Excellence

Celebrating Black Excellence

Shown in picture (left to right): Derrick Johnson, President & CEO, NAACP, Will Alston - Head of Corporate Banking, Corporate & Investment Banking Executive, Wells Fargo, Brenda Lauderback - Board Chair, Denny’s, Buwa Binitie - Founder & CEO, Dantes Partners, LLC.

Our inaugural Black Business Leaders’ Summit was held recently in New Orleans. Prominent Black Business leaders gathered to engage in meaningful conversations around common goals and building a more inclusive community. 

Bloomberg radio was on-site to speak with our Wells Fargo leaders and attendees.

  • Will Alston, Managing Director, Head of Corporate Banking, Corporate & Investment Banking, Wells Fargo

  • Melissa Bradley, Founder and Managing Partner, 1863 Ventures

  • Kristy Fercho, Head of Diverse Segments, Representation, and Inclusion, Wells Fargo
