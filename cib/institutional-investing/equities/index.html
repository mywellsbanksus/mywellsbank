When you work with Wells Fargo you get the best of both worlds: access to the comprehensive resources and capabilities of a full-service brokerage firm and the high-touch service of a boutique investment house. Our equities sales, trading, and investment professionals provide market insights, customized investment options, and a range of execution strategies for issuing and investing customers.

Our integrated service model supports the trading, financing, liquidity access and market-making needs of institutional investors, corporates, and distributors and covers the full universe of U.S. stocks. Our continually expanding platform delivers a range of equity and equity-linked products and services with professional execution and a customer-centric focus.

Cash equities

We provide resources and services to leverage trade flows from one of the nation's largest distribution networks. This provides customers access to customized execution and support:

  • High-touch cash trading
  • WATS® Equities Electronic Trading
  • Exchange traded funds
  • Outsourced trading

Equity derivatives

We offer the strategies and tools to help customers manage equity exposure, customize trading structures, and support specific strategies through:

  • Convertibles
  • Synthetics
  • Flow derivatives
  • Corporates
  • Structured products

We offer a variety of ways to manage equity risk.

Structured Investment Group

We work with you to express an investment view through a variety of investment vehicles including:

  • Structured notes, special-purpose vehicles, OTC contracts, market linked investments, and separate accounts

Prime Services

Our Prime Services team provides integrated financing, technology, operational and custody services supporting the needs of alternative asset managers from emerging to large institutional funds.

  • Integrated financing solutions
  • Technology and operational solutions
  • Capital introductions
  • Business consulting services
  • Risk management solutions

Corporate access

Our Corporate Access team provides customized services that bring together investors and management teams, cultivating information flow and developing relationship opportunities for both corporate clients and investors.
