Technology investment banking – leadership team

Brian Gudofsky

Brian Gudofsky is a Managing Director and the Head of Technology, Media and Telecom Corporate and Investment Banking at Wells Fargo. He joined the team in 2023. Prior to joining Wells Fargo, Brian was the Global Head of Technology at a leading Investment Bank, where he spent over two decades building premiere coverage and M&A businesses. Brian spent his first 13 years in banking in M&A, including running a Tech M&A business before moving to coverage, focusing on driving strategic dialogue across multiple industry verticals. Brian also established and grew the FinTech and Payments business at his prior firm into a leading practice. In addition to his corporate clients, Brian has extensive relationships across the financial sponsor community and has advised on numerous marquee transactions. Brian holds MBA/Law degrees from the University of Toronto and is based in New York today.

Gerry Walters

Gerry Walters is a Managing Director and the Head of Technology Corporate and Investment Banking at Wells Fargo. He joined the team in 2010. Gerry has more than 30 years of securities industry experience and has spent the last 25 years as a technology investment banker based in the San Francisco Bay Area. He previously worked as a Managing Director at J.P. Morgan and Hambrecht & Quist. Gerry started his career as an attorney with the Corporate Securities group at Gray Cary (now DLA Piper) and in the Corporate Finance department of the U.S. Securities and Exchange Commission. Gerry has extensive experience across capital markets (equity and debt) and M&A and a diverse set of client relationships across the entire technology sector.

Joe Greeves

Joe Greeves is a Managing Director and the Co-Head of Software Investment Banking at Wells Fargo. He joined the team in 2023 from Bank of America, where he supported their TMT investment banking priorities for almost 15 years. Joe has originated and executed multiple advisory and capital raising transactions for software and private equity clients including lead-left IPOs, equity follow-on offerings, convertible notes offerings, and high-yield offerings. Joe also has extensive experience advising on both the buy- and sell-side for clients across a diverse array of software sub-sectors. Joe is based in New York.
