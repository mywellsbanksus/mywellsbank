Our REIT finance team delivers corporate banking products and services to the public and private Real Estate Investment Trust (REIT) industry, including corporate-level lending, asset-level lending and capital structure advisory.  We utilize our deep REIT expertise and substantial capital commitment to the industry to deliver capabilities across the entire Wells Fargo organization, meeting client needs efficiently.

Customers:

  • Publicly traded and private REITs

Our products include:

  • Unsecured revolvers
  • Term loans
  • Select bridge opportunities
  • Secured standing loans
  • Construction loans

We can also help you with the following services:

  • Fixed income management
  • Interest rate risk management
  • Common and preferred equity management
  • Treasury management
  • Wealth management
