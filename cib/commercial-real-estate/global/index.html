Canada and EMEA Commercial Real Estate Lending

  • Print

Commercial real estate financing solutions offered in Canada and the U.K.

Wells Fargo offers financing for all stages of commercial real estate projects in Canada and the United Kingdom.

Canada

Our local commercial real estate teams bring the full spectrum of Wells Fargo commercial real estate services to Canadian developers and U.S.-based developers with Canadian projects.

Learn more about the full range of Wells Fargo commercial real estate financial products and services available in Canada.

United Kingdom and Republic of Ireland

Our teams are based in London and Dublin to cover all aspects of commercial real estate lending.

  • Our Products
  • Our Team
  • Pan-UK and Republic of Ireland coverage
  • Finance for real estate asset sectors including living, commercial, and hospitality
  • Loan sizes typically greater than 20 million GBP
  • A wide range of lending products, including
    • Investment finance
    • Development finance
    • Corporate facilities
    • Floating and fixed rate loans
    • ESG finance
    • Loan-on-loan finance
    • Syndicated loan facilities

We also offer access to a range of associated products and services to real estate customers, including debt capital markets, private placements, fund finance, and derivatives.

Our focus on UK and Irish real estate enables us to be specialists in the sector combining local market expertise with the size and strength of Wells Fargo and its long established real estate franchise.

We work closely with the Wells Fargo global business to provide our customers with comprehensive financial solutions.
