The Multifamily Capital (WFMC) production team specializes in working with Fannie Mae and Freddie Mac (Government Sponsored Enterprises or GSEs), Federal Housing Administration (FHA) and the WFMC balance sheet to originate financing solutions for multifamily assets including conventional, affordable, student housing,  seniors housing, manufactured home communities (MHC) and healthcare facilities. For specific questions regarding Multifamily Capital, please connect with one of our originators.
