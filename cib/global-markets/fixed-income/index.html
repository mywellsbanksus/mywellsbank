Commodities

Commodities

Benefit from our expertise in managing price risks across major commodity classes such as agriculture, metals, and energy. Whether you prefer traditional hedging strategies or physical transactions. We support commodity producers and users in managing price exposure effectively. Rely on our capability to help you navigate and mitigate risks across a broad spectrum of commodities.

  • Agriculture: Wells Fargo provides food and agribusiness price risk management and repo financing on agriculture products to producers, processors, cooperatives, restaurant companies, and more.
  • Metals: Bank customers with metals exposure have access to our tailored suite of products used to manage commodity price risk and monetize inventory, strengthening consumer, producer, and trader balance sheets.
  • Energy: In partnership with the Energy banking team, the commodity products group delivers top-tier financial and physical energy solutions to upstream, midstream, and diversified consumers.

Foreign exchange

Leverage our global expertise, local coverage, and unique quantitative analytics to effectively manage your FX risks and payment needs. Our platform facilitates transactions in 120 currencies, offering multinational corporate clients a range of foreign currency services and solutions, including:

Interest rate risk management

Interest rate risk management

Collaborate with our interest rate hedging specialists to design and implement tailored hedging strategies aligned with your financial goals. Our expertise supports you with a variety of services and solutions, including:

  • Risk assessment: We help you identify and quantify your interest rate risk so complementary hedging strategies can be developed.
  • Hedging strategies: We provide education and analysis of various hedging strategies based on your preferences and risk tolerance.
