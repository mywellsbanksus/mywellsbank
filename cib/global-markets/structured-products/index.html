Structured Products

Discover tailored structured financing solutions for your investments

Comprehensive asset-backed services

Our Structured Products group delivers comprehensive asset-backed services, including structured lending, underwriting, and distribution of new issue securities, offering seamless access for domestic and international clients and partners to tailored financial solutions.

Asset-backed securities

Asset-backed securities

We offer a range of financial solutions tailored to benefit our clients’ specific needs and goals.

Financing flexibility: ABS&L provides structured, non-recourse balance sheet lending solutions (ABS) and recourse financing (ABL) to support clients funding needs in the form of traditional warehouse lines to term securitization, permanent core warehouse financing / term loans, and corporate credit facilities.

Capital markets expertise: As a top five player in capital markets solutions, ABS&L specializes in public and private term ABS securitization. ABS&L’s deep understanding of assets finance provides insight to both our issuer clients and institutional investors so that our clients can navigate the market with confidence.

Corporate debt finance

Corporate debt finance

We specialize in delivering structured finance and securitization solutions to investors and asset managers.

Structured financing: CDF allocates significant balance sheet capital to provide term-based lending against middle-market and broadly syndicated loan collateral, as well as warehouse lending for efficient collateralized loan obligation (CLO) execution.

Capital market leadership: As a leading player in capital markets solutions, CDF excels in delivering middle market and broadly syndicated CLOs. Our track record of strong performance and global marketing strategy focuses on institutional investors, resulting in market performance that benefited our clients.

Mortgage finance

Mortgage finance

We provide warehouse and structured financing and capital markets solutions to clients that own, originate, or invest in residential real estate loans and securities.

Lending: Benefit from highly structured secured lending arrangements collateralized by a variety of assets including residential MBS, residential whole loans, servicing advance receivables, single-family rental, short term investor loans, early buyout, and agency and jumbo loans.

Capital markets: Access expert advisory services, securities underwriting, and distribution channels for clients seeking entry into bond markets via RMBS issuance.

Work with MFG to leverage our specialized expertise and customized solutions that are designed to empower your company to thrive in the dynamic landscape of residential real estate finance.
